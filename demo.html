How Outage Continuity Works

1. Secure Data Ingestion

We securely take your agency's run cards, emergency parameters, and jurisdictional response grids directly from spreadsheets or GIS data, transforming them into light, structured system profiles.

2. Local Caching Deployment

Dispatchers download the platform onto their desktop consoles as a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once logged in, the application safely caches all mission-critical parameters directly into local, sandboxed storage.

3. Fail-Safe Outage Readiness

When primary network links drop, vendor servers fail, or your CAD disconnects completely, the PWA stays live. Because data is processed entirely on the workstation floor, search and determinants remain completely active.
