About Outage Response

Outage Response is designed to support 911 centers and emergency dispatch teams during critical system outages. Our goal is to ensure that emergency services can maintain communication, access essential response plans, and coordinate resources efficiently even when traditional systems fail.

What We Do

During unexpected outages, every second counts. Outage Response provides a centralized platform where dispatchers can quickly:

  • Access pre-configured response plans and protocols
  • Coming Soon: Locate the correct fire, EMS, and police districts for any address
  • Communicate with partner agencies and field users when primary systems are down
  • Maintain operational continuity without relying on disrupted systems

Our Mission

We understand that outages can be chaotic. Our mission is to minimize the impact on emergency response and keep public safety operations running smoothly. By providing a reliable backup platform tailored to the unique needs of 911 centers, Outage Response empowers dispatchers to make informed decisions under pressure.

Who We Serve

Outage Response is built specifically for 911 dispatch centers, emergency operations coordinators, and first responder agencies who need a dependable solution during system failures.
