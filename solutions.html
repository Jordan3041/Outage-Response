How It Works

1. Primary CAD Failure

Your primary Computer-Aided Dispatch system experiences unexpected downtime or loss of connectivity.

2. Instant PWA Activation

Dispatchers open the OutageResponse Web App on any device. It boots instantly, reading secure data directly from local storage.

3. Continuous Operations

Personnel handle determinant calculations, coordinate mutual aid cross-references, and track jurisdictions seamlessly without paper maps.

4. Primary System Restored

Once your network or primary software architecture stabilizes, telemetry data reconciles back without a breakdown in service delivery.
